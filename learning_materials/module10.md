Learn to design ALM processes for AI solutions, ensuring governance, security, and consistency across environments.

## Learning objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Design ALM processes for AI components like datasets, prompts, and models.
- Establish governance, security, and monitoring across environments.
- Ensure consistent and compliant AI behavior from development to production.

## Prerequisites

- Familiarity with AI concepts and lifecycle management.
- Basic understanding of Microsoft technologies like Copilot Studio, Dynamics 365, and Azure AI.
