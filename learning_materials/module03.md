Learn to design an enterprise AI strategy using Azure's Cloud Adoption Framework, aligning AI agent lifecycle with business goals for operational excellence.

## Learning objectives

After completing this module, you'll be able to:

- Map CAF's AI adoption phases to the AI agent lifecycle.
- Design an enterprise-ready operating model for AI agents.
- Select and apply Microsoft platforms for AI solutions.
- Utilize checklists to transition AI workloads from proof of concept to production.

## Prerequisites

- Familiarity with Azure Cloud Adoption Framework.
- Basic understanding of AI agent lifecycle and governance.
